25 Data-Driven Questions for the Olist SQL Project

1. Sales & Revenue Analysis
	1.	What is the total revenue generated per year and per month?
	2.	Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
	3.	What are the top 10 most profitable products?
	4.	Which states or cities generate the most sales revenue?
	5.	How much revenue does each seller generate on average?

2. Customer Behavior Analysis
	1.	Which customers placed the highest number of orders?
	2.	What is the average number of orders per customer?
	3.	Which states have the highest number of customers?
	4.	What percentage of customers are repeat buyers?
	5.	What is the average order value (AOV) per customer?

3. Product Performance
	1.	Which product categories are purchased the most?
	2.	Which product categories receive the highest average review score?
	3.	Which product categories receive the lowest ratings?

4. Seller Performance
	1.	Which sellers have the highest total revenue?
	2.	Which sellers deliver orders the fastest?
	3.	Which sellers receive the best customer review ratings?

	1.	What is the average delivery time from purchase to delivery?
